voice blaster platinum

  • how it works
  • command cheatsheet
  • get it

  1. after you have created and named a clip, use the name as a command to tell voice blaster platinum to play it back.

  3. audio commands

  4. ;clip (user)
    select a segment of audio to save
  5. ;delete (title)
    delete a clip permanently!
  6. ;download (title)
    download a clip for your own purposes
  7. ;gtfo
    tell the bot to leave a voice channel
  8. ;list
    list all the clips you can play
  9. ;listen
    undo ;stop listening
  10. ;nuke
    clear all queued-up clips
  11. ;random
    play a random clip
  12. ;repeat (title) (number) times
    hear a clip multiple times
  13. ;stop listening
    stops the bot from listening to voice chat
  14. ;welcome (user) with (clip)
    play a clip whenever someone joins voice chat

  16. housekeeping

  17. ;admin (user)
    give someone admin privileges
  18. ;ban (user)
    cut a user off from Voice Blaster Platinum
  19. ;share
    get the bot add link
  20. ;sync
    tell the bot check for new clips
  21. ;unadmin (user)
    undoes ;admin
  22. ;unban (user)
    undoes ;ban
